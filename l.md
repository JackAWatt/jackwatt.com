$\mu = 340$

$\sigma = 40$
---

$\frac {360 - 340}{40} = 0.5$

$Z(0.5) = 0.69146$

$\frac {460-340}{40} = 3$

$Z(3) = 0.99865$

$Z(3) - Z(0.5) = 0.99865 - 0.69146 = 0.30719$

$5000 * 0.30719 = 1535.95$

> It's expected that 1536 out of 5000 batteries will last between 360 and 460 days.
